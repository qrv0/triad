## Three classes of correspondence

The nine mappings divide into three structurally distinct classes by evidentiary type.

**Mathematical equivalence (no calibration).** This is the strongest possible type of cross-domain correspondence: the formal mathematical structures are identical. Two instances fall into this class. The first is the family of NLS instantiations ([`01-other-nls-systems.md`](01-other-nls-systems.md): BEC, optics, oceanography): the equation derived here is the same equation that already describes those systems, with the addition of memory and FDT-locked noise as structural extensions. The second is the state space model correspondence ([`06-state-space-models.md`](06-state-space-models.md)): the auxiliary-field memory $\partial_t y_j = \nu_j(\rho - y_j)$ is literally the diagonal-state SSM update used in machine learning. No physical units bridge the two formulations; the equations are the same equation.

**Calibration-dependent structural correspondence.** This is the substantive intermediate type: the mathematical structure is the same form, but the absolute scales at which the form appears in each domain are set by domain-specific dimensional choices. Four mappings fall into this class. [`02-baryon-acoustic.md`](02-baryon-acoustic.md) (BAO) places the acoustic dynamics of the photon-baryon plasma in the equation's wave-in-coupled-medium structure. [`03-chladni-cymatics.md`](03-chladni-cymatics.md) places spontaneous geometric pattern formation in vibrated continuous media in the same modulational-instability family as the equation's BCC selection. [`04-gamma-entrainment.md`](04-gamma-entrainment.md) places the equation's broadband-absorption regime, under one calibration, in the gamma band where neural entrainment effects are documented. [`05-archaeoacoustic-resonance.md`](05-archaeoacoustic-resonance.md) places the equation's two principal vibrational modes, under one calibration, at 66 Hz and 111 Hz where megalithic chambers and a documented EEG response operate. The structural fact, that the equation has the structures these substrates exhibit, is preserved across the domains; the absolute frequencies, wavelengths, and coupling strengths are calibrated separately in each domain to its specific physical scale.

**Mechanism-shape correspondence and convergent-program correspondence.** Three mappings fall into this third class, where the structural correspondence is at the level of mechanism shape (the trajectory the system traces) or convergent observation (an independent research program documenting the same phenomenology the structural argument predicts), without committing to a specific dimensional calibration. [`07-cosmological-expansion.md`](07-cosmological-expansion.md) identifies the cosmological trajectory (concentrated initial state, inflationary release, structure formation, sustained expansion) as the same trajectory the equation produces in laboratory anti-collapse simulation, at a scale separated by approximately 60 orders of magnitude in length. [`08-mechanistic-interpretability.md`](08-mechanistic-interpretability.md) is a convergent-program correspondence: the structural argument predicts that an architecture lacking P2's auxiliary-field memory hierarchy (attention-only) must encode categorical structure as superposed projections, which is what the Anthropic mech interp program independently documents. [`09-critical-brain.md`](09-critical-brain.md) is similarly convergent: the broadband-multi-timescale phenomenology that the critical-brain literature (Beggs-Plenz, Chialvo, Mora-Bialek) tracks in cortex is the phenomenology the equation produces in its broadband-absorbing crystalline regime.

The three classes carry different evidentiary weights. A mathematical equivalence is harder to dismiss than a calibrated structural correspondence: the latter can always be questioned on the choice of calibration; the former cannot. A mechanism-shape or convergent-program correspondence is the lightest evidentiary weight on a per-mapping basis but accumulates across independent research programs in a way that calibration-dependent mappings do not. All three classes contribute to the structural-realist position; the cross-domain coherence criterion (methodology/04 criterion 4) operates on their cumulative weight.

## Why this folder is the structural argument

A standard machine-learning repository or physics paper buries cross-domain content in an introduction or a discussion section. This work places it at the same hierarchical level as the equation itself. The decision reflects the methodological position documented in [`../methodology/`](../methodology/): a structural theory is evaluated not by single-quantity predictive precision in one domain but by the coherence of its form across multiple domains. The cross-domain mappings are not appendix material; they are first-class content equal in weight to the mathematical derivation and the numerical results.

A reader who has reached this folder has the right context to evaluate the work as it asks to be evaluated: not "does this equation predict X with isolated precision?" but "does the same structural form appear in independently documented physical, biological, and computational systems, and if so what does that imply?" Each document in this folder is one piece of evidence for the answer.
