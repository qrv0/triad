# For philosophers of science

> An attempt to operationalize structural realism into a concrete worked example — one that brings the tension between P3 (coupling) and Popperian falsificationism into the open rather than resolving it quietly.

> *The load-bearing object of knowledge is the mathematical structure.*
> *The cross-domain co-occurrence of the structure is the principal evidence for the structural-realist claim.*

---

## What this maps to in your area

The equation is derived from three structural axioms (P1 oscillation, P2 self-reference, P3 coupling). P3 — *coupling is the default; isolation is temporary* — is in direct structural tension with Popperian falsificationism, which presupposes the isolability of variables. A theory whose third axiom denies isolation cannot consistently be evaluated by a methodology that presupposes it; this motivates the six-criteria framework in [`methodology/04-the-six-criteria.md`](../methodology/04-the-six-criteria.md). The cross-domain interfaces range from exact mathematical equivalence (no calibration: SSM, Kuramoto, other NLS systems) through calibration-dependent structural correspondence (gamma entrainment, archaeoacoustic resonance) to convergent-program correspondence (critical brain, Friston FEP, mechanistic interpretability), with explicit per-class discussion of evidentiary weight.
